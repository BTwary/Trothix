TERMS OF SERVICE
Section 12. Force Majeure.
Neither party shall be liable for any delay or failure in performance under this Agreement due to acts of God, flood, fire, earthquake, war, strikes, or governmental order, provided that the affected party gives prompt written notice to the other party. If the delay exceeds sixty days, either party may terminate.

Section 15. Governing Law.
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Florida.
