# Definitions Clause

## Purpose

Provide authoritative meanings for words, phrases, and legal concepts used
throughout the agreement to ensure consistent interpretation, reduce ambiguity,
and establish the scope of contractual rights and obligations.

Definitions are foundational. Nearly every operative clause relies on them.

Uses "means."

---

# Missing Component Risks

Missing Definitions

Risk

Undefined obligations.

Recommendation

Define every capitalized legal term.

---

Missing Scope

Risk

Conflicting interpretation.

Recommendation

Clarify whether the definition applies globally or locally.

---

Open Definition Without Limits

Risk

Unexpected expansion.

Recommendation

Limit open-ended definitions where appropriate.

---

Circular Definition

Risk

Impossible interpretation.

Recommendation

Avoid definitions that depend on themselves.

---

Conflicting Definitions

Risk

Multiple meanings.

Recommendation

Use one canonical definition.

# Jurisdiction Notes

## US

Detailed defined terms.

Heavy use of "means."

Frequently uses expansive commercial definitions.

---

## UK

Definitions are generally narrower.

Greater reliance on interpretation principles.

---

## India

Definitions often reference applicable statutes.

Commercial drafting favors explicit wording.

---

## Australia

Modern plain-English definitions.

Less redundancy while preserving precision.

---

## EU

Frequently incorporates statutory definitions,
especially for privacy and consumer law.
