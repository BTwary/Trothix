# Acceptance Clause

## Purpose

Define the process, criteria, timing, and consequences of accepting goods,
services, software, or deliverables under the agreement.

Acceptance determines when contractual performance is considered complete and
when related rights and obligations begin.

# Missing Component Risks

Missing Acceptance Criteria

Risk

Approval becomes subjective.

Recommendation

Define objective acceptance standards.

---

Missing Testing Period

Risk

Uncertain acceptance timing.

Recommendation

Specify testing window.

---

Missing Cure Process

Risk

No structured defect resolution.

Recommendation

Require repair or reperformance process.

---

Missing Deemed Acceptance Rule

Risk

Deliverables may remain perpetually unaccepted.

Recommendation

Specify automatic acceptance conditions.
