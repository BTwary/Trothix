# Interpretation Clause

## Purpose

Define the rules used to interpret contractual language, resolve ambiguity,
and determine the intended legal meaning of the agreement.

Interpretation clauses reduce disputes by establishing common construction
principles before disagreements arise.

# Standard Interpretation Rules

Common principles

- Definitions control ordinary meaning.
- Specific provisions override general provisions.
- Later amendments override earlier text.
- Mandatory law overrides inconsistent contractual language.
- Cross-references must be resolved before interpretation.
- Carve-outs modify the rule they qualify.
- Exceptions narrow obligations.
- Defined terms retain the meaning assigned in the Definitions section.
- Headings do not affect interpretation unless expressly stated.
