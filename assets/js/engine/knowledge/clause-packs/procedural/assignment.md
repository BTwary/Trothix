# Assignment Clause

## Purpose

Define whether contractual rights and obligations may be transferred,
delegated, assigned, or novated to another person or entity.

The clause determines who may become a party to the agreement after execution.

# Missing Component Risks

Missing Consent

Risk

Unexpected transfer of contractual rights.

Recommendation

Define when consent is required.

---

Missing Change of Control

Risk

Corporate acquisitions may bypass commercial expectations.

Recommendation

Address mergers and acquisitions explicitly.

---

Missing Liability Rule

Risk

Responsibility after assignment becomes uncertain.

Recommendation

Clarify continuing liability.

---

Missing Affiliate Exception

Risk

Routine internal restructuring becomes unnecessarily difficult.

Recommendation

Permit assignments within corporate groups where appropriate.
