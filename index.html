How Trothix Works

Seven deterministic layers working in concert to structure legal reasoning.

Parser +

Transforms unstructured document text into a clean token stream.

Our custom lexical analyzer cleans raw input text, normalizes spacing variations, isolates structural headings, list bullet points, and signatures, preparing it for compiler-style AST conversion.

Legal AST +

Constructs an Abstract Syntax Tree mapping legal syntax.

The AST breaks down clauses into discrete node hierarchies. It maps active verbs, conditional phrases, exceptions, and lists of obligations, isolating terms for logical evaluation.

Ontology +

Defines the vocabulary of roles, deadlines, and rights.

Maps natural language entities to standard concepts (e.g. "Notice Window", "Cure Period", or "Indemnity Cap"), ensuring terms are classified with exact taxonomic precision.

Rule Engine +

Applies deterministic code-logic checks against the AST.

Evaluates a suite of rules defined as code. Check conditions (e.g. "if cure period < 30 days") are computed with 100% mathematical certainty, eliminating AI model drift and hallucination risk.

Knowledge Provider +

Injects standard guidelines and baseline benchmarks.

Provides comparative standard templates, default statutory values, and common commercial baselines to highlight deviations, non-standard clauses, and missing elements.

Reasoning Engine +

Traces dependencies and compound obligations.

Traces the cross-relations between clauses—resolving how termination notice windows intersect with renewal milestones, or whether a limitation of liability applies to indemnities.

Explanation Generator +

Explains risks and findings in clear, plain English.

Translates logical check results and AST anomalies into clean, human-readable explanations. It answers the question "why is this a risk?" using strictly grounded logic nodes.
