MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT Effective: March 1, 2024  ·  Docket: MSA-2024-001

This Agreement is entered into by ACME Corp ("Company") and Beta Inc. ("Client").

1. DEFINITIONS
"Confidential Information" means non‑public data disclosed by either party. "Deliverables" means the software and documentation specified in SOWs.

2. OBLIGATIONS
Company shall provide the Trothix platform and support. Client shall pay fees per the SOW.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party agrees to hold the other's Confidential Information in strict confidence and not to disclose it to any third party except as required by law.

4. INDEMNIFICATION
Company shall indemnify Client against claims arising from Company's gross negligence or willful misconduct. Client shall indemnify Company against claims arising from Client's use of the Deliverables.

5. AUTOMATIC RENEWAL
This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one‑year terms unless either party provides written notice of non‑renewal at least 60 days prior to expiration.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California, without regard to conflict of laws principles.

7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
In no event shall either party be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. Total liability shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client in the 12 months preceding the claim.
