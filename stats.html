Usage stats

In-memory, per-instance counters — these reset on cold start and aren't a durable all-time total (see api/_stats.js). Good enough to sanity-check traffic and error rates during early usage; swap in a persistent store before relying on this for real reporting. "Page views" counts page loads, not unique visitors — a reload or a repeat visit both add to it. "Total analysis requests" counts every hit to /api/analyze — including your own testing, retried 502s, and any direct API calls — so it can legitimately be higher than page views.
