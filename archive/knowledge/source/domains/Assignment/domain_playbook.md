# Domain Playbook: Assignment

## Purpose
The Assignment domain governs the transfer of rights or obligations under the contract. It evaluates delegation boundaries, consent triggers, and exceptions for mergers or affiliate restructuring.

## Common Legal Risks
- Allowing assignment without prior written consent.
- Failing to block delegation of duties.
- Missing change-of-control triggers.

## Current Knowledge Debt
- Missing phrase patterns: Delegation limits in joint ventures.
- Future priorities: Successor-in-interest rights.
