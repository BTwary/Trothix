# Domain Playbook: Notice

## Purpose
The Notice domain captures how communications must be transmitted between parties, ensuring legal validity of actions like Termination and Breach claims.

## Common Drafting Styles
- Broad multi-channel notice.
- Strict physical delivery notice.
- Email-only notice.

## Required Concepts
- NOTICE_METHOD
- NOTICE_ADDRESS
- NOTICE_DEEMED_RECEIVED
- NOTICE_BUSINESS_DAY

## Common Legal Risks
- Failing to include an effective address.
- Permitting email without requiring read-receipt or confirmation.
- Undefined "Business Day".

## Current Knowledge Debt
- Missing Phrase Families: Permitted Disclosures cross-links.
- Missing Jurisdictions: EU/UK GDPR variations.
- Future priorities: Severability clauses, Cure Periods.
